Scares on a Budget


Indie (or independent movies) are films made outside of the major studio system, are often low-budget, and stick closer to the director’s personal artistic vision. This gives room for more ideas and creativity. The best indie films are the ones that are of good quality despite the budget and circumstances surrounding production.

That’s why genre movies such as horror have thrived in the independent scene. Horror films need to get creative to get under your skin, so it’s fitting that the most influential are the most experimental, and thus the most shocking. That’s why provocative movies such as Texas Chainsaw Massacre get made and get to influence the horror genre; because there is more freedom and need for resourcefulness in indie cinema.
